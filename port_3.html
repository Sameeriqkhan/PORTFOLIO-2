Water Bottle Holder

This project showcases a uniquely designed water bottle holder, tailored to improve portability and compatibility with various bottle sizes. The holder was designed using SolidWorks and optimized for 3D printing, taking into account durability, ergonomic grip, and ease of access.

Key constraints included wall thickness, print time, and performance during stress tests to ensure the design could reliably secure bottles during movement or while attached to bags. I iterated through several design prototypes before finalizing a model that passed drop tests and field usage scenarios.

The final product is lightweight, durable, and adjustable, providing a simple yet effective solution for day-to-day convenience and accessibility.
