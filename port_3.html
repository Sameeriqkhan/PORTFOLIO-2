Overview

In this project, I was tasked with creating a unique and versatile water bottle sleeve that incorporated specific features. The primary focus was to design a sleeve that not only held the water bottle securely but also provided additional functionalities to enhance user convenience. One of the key features of the water bottle sleeve was the phone holding portion. The aim was to create a secure and snug space where users could keep their phones while carrying the water bottle. By carefully considering the dimensions and ergonomics, I ensured that the phone would be held tightly, minimizing the risk of accidental slips or damage. Another important aspect of the design was the card holding slot. Understanding the need for practicality and organization, I incorporated a slot that allowed users to store their cards within the sleeve. This feature eliminated the need for carrying a separate wallet, providing a convenient all-in-one solution for users on the go. To further enhance the functionality of the water bottle sleeve, I integrated an Apple AirTag holder. This additional feature enabled users to track the sleeve's location at any point, giving them peace of mind and an added layer of security. By carefully considering the user's needs and incorporating these specific features, I created a water bottle sleeve that offers convenience, organization, and peace of mind in one compact design. The result is a versatile product that caters to the modern lifestyle, combining practicality and functionality in an aesthetically pleasing manner.
